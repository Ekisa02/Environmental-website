💚 Who We Are

Eldoret Green Future Alliance (EGFA) is a youth-led environmental organization dedicated to restoring, protecting, and advocating for a greener Eldoret and beyond.

🎯 Our Mission

To mobilize communities, empower youth, and implement sustainable environmental solutions that secure a cleaner, healthier future for all.

🌍 Our Vision

A green and thriving Eldoret where nature and humanity coexist in harmony.

🌿 Our Story

Founded in 2023 by students at the University of Eldoret, EGFA began with tree-planting drives. Today, we’re a growing movement tackling climate change, pollution, and land degradation across Uasin Gishu.

🛠️ What We Do

  • 🌳 Tree planting and reforestation
  • 🧹 Clean-up campaigns
  • 📚 Climate education
  • 📢 Policy advocacy
  • 📱 Green tech innovation

💎 Our Core Values

  • Sustainability: We care for nature like our future depends on it
  • Community: Change is stronger when it’s shared
  • Transparency: We lead with honesty
  • Action: We believe in doing, not just talking

👥 Meet Our Team

📌 Green Schools Tree Planting & Eco-Club Launch – Uasin Gishu Primary

Description: This was the first official project by Eldoret Green Future Eco-Alliance (EGFEA), launched on 11th July 2025 at Uasin Gishu Primary School. It involved two key activities — a large-scale tree planting campaign and the establishment of the school’s first environmental club. The eco-club will serve as a long-term platform for student-led sustainability activities.

Key Achievements:

  • 🌳 267 trees planted within the school compound
  • 👩‍🏫 Eco-club officially launched with students and teachers
  • 👥 100+ pupils, teachers, and volunteers engaged
  • 🧠 Environmental and mental health sensitization sessions
  • 🏢 Supported by Diesel Power Company Limited
